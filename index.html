We Answer.

  • How long do results last with NUDY. Coco Wax?
  • The wax is designed to provide long-lasting results and the duration may vary from person to person. On average, you can enjoy smooth, hair-free skin for up to four weeks.
  • How soon can I strip the hair after application?
  • Enjoy the convenience of swift hair removal. With NUDY. Coco Wax, you can strip the hair instantly after application for instant gratification.
  • Is NUDY. Coco Wax painful to use?
  • Not at all! Our wax is formulated for low-pain application, ensuring a pleasurable hair removal experience without compromising on effectiveness.
  • How often should I use NUDY. Coco Wax?
  • For optimal results, use the wax every 3-4 weeks, depending on your hair growth. P.S. Our wax will decrease the growth rate with the waxing period being once every two months for different target zones.
  • Is NUDY. Coco Wax suitable for sensitive skin?
  • Absolutely! Our wax is formulated to be gentle on all skin types, including sensitive skin.
  • Can I use NUDY. Coco Wax on my face?
  • NO! Our wax is primarily designed for body use. If a customer decides to use the wax on their face, it’s of their own volition and at their own risk.
  • Is NUDY. Wax Heater easy to clean?
  • Yes, the wax heater is designed with silicone for easy cleaning. Simply wait for the wax to cool down and dry then remove the leftover pieces. We recommend cleaning the silicone heater with alcohol as a finishing touch.
  • Can I clean NUDY. Wax Heater in the dishwasher?
  • Absolutely NOT! The heater is made of silicone, if you place it in the dishwasher it will damage the material and make it unusable.
  • How often can I use NUDY. Wax Heater?
  • The wax heater is durable for regular use. We recommend using it whenever you desire smooth, hair-free skin. NB. Ensure it’s thoroughly cleaned after each use.
